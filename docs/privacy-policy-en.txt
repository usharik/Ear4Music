Privacy Policy for Ear4Music

Last updated: 2026-03-11

Ear4Music is an offline ear-training app.

1. Personal data
The app does not require account registration and does not intentionally collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or microphone recordings.

2. Internet and third parties
The app does not request Internet permission in the current version and does not include advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting services.

3. Data stored on your device
The app may store training-related information on your device, such as exercise progress, results, favourites, and app preferences.

4. Backups
Android system backup is enabled for the app. Depending on your device and Google backup settings, locally stored app data may be included in system backups.

5. Data deletion
You can delete the app data at any time in Android settings or by uninstalling the app.

6. Changes to this policy
This policy may be updated if the app functionality changes.
