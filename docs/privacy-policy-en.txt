Privacy Policy for Ear4Music

Last updated: 2026-03-11

Ear4Music is an ear-training app. Its learning features work locally on your device, and the current version also includes banner advertising.

1. Personal data
The app does not require account registration and does not intentionally collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, or microphone recordings.

2. Internet access
The current version of the app requests Internet and network state access to load and display banner ads.

3. Advertising and third-party services
The app uses Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) to display banner ads. When ads are requested or shown, Google and its partners may process technical and advertising-related data according to their own privacy policies, for example device identifiers, IP address, approximate location derived from IP, diagnostics, and ad interaction data. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob documentation.

4. Data stored on your device
The app may store training-related information on your device, such as exercise progress, results, favourites, and app preferences.

5. Backups
Android system backup is enabled for the app. Depending on your device and Google backup settings, locally stored app data may be included in system backups.

6. Data deletion
You can delete the app data at any time in Android settings or by uninstalling the app.

7. Changes to this policy
This policy may be updated if the app functionality changes.
